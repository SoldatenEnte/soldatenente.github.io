Hue Together

A 2D Cooperative Puzzle-Platformer

← Back to Portfolio

Project Overview

The vision for Hue Together is to create a delightful and engaging two-player cooperative puzzle-platformer, reminiscent of classics like _Fireboy & Watergirl_. As a group project for a university "Mediaengineering" course, it was designed to challenge players' communication and teamwork skills through intuitive, color-based mechanics. Its charming and tangible "desktop world" art style, where characters and levels are made of paper and office supplies, aims to provide a warm and accessible experience for all ages.

Key Features

  • Two-Player Co-op System: Built from the ground up for local two-player gameplay with controls for two distinct characters.
  • Color-Based Puzzle Mechanics: The central gameplay loop is fully implemented. Each player controls a character of a specific color and can only interact with objects that match their own color.
  • Platforming Movement: Standard 2D platformer controls (moving and jumping) are functional, allowing players to navigate the level.
  • Custom Art and Animation: A complete set of custom-designed art assets and animations has been created and integrated, establishing the game's unique papercraft aesthetic.
  • Interactive Elements: The game world includes essential puzzle components like color-coded keys and locked doors, which are key to level progression.
